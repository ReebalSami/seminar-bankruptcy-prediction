Modeling Recommendations

Temporal Holdout Validation Strategy

  • Training Set: H1 + H2 + H3 (early horizons)
  • Validation Set: H4 (tune hyperparameters)
  • Test Set: H5 (final evaluation)

Why this approach?

  • Respects temporal ordering
  • Mimics real-world prediction scenario
  • Tests model generalization to longer horizons
  • Prevents data leakage from future to past
